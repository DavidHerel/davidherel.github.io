I am a PhD student in the Computer Science doctoral study program at the Department of Cybernetics, Faculty of Electrical Engineering (FEE), Czech Technical University (CTU) in Prague. I am researching Novel Methods in Natural Language Processing under the supervision of Tomas Mikolov.
I am the third winner of prestigious Alexa Prize Socialbot Grand Challenge 5. I regularly contribute to top-tier conferences in the AI&NLP field (ICLR, ECAI, Amazon Science). My diploma thesis has been honored with Dean's award nominations, and my research has garnered esteemed recognitions (grants, interviews).
