Hi, I’m David – a hands-on founder and systems builder with deep experience in efficient AI. At BottleCap AI, I co-lead a mission to make large language models radically more efficient, backed by a $10M founding round and a team obsessed with performance. Before this, I built algorithmic trading systems from scratch at Hive Spirit Technologies and led winning teams in Amazon’s Alexa Prize Grand Challenge. My AI research has been published at top conferences, and I left my PhD just before finishing to fully commit to building real-world AI.
