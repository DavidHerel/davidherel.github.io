I am a PhD student in the Computer Science doctoral study program at the Department of Cybernetics, Faculty of Electrical Engineering (FEE), Czech Technical University (CTU) in Prague. I am researching Novel Methods in Natural Language Processing under the supervision of Tomas Mikolov.
Previously, I got a bachelor's and master's degree from FEE CTU, both in computer science. My diploma thesis was nominated for a Dean's award. Before starting my doctoral studies, I was an AI researcher at the Czech Institute of Informatics, Robotics and Cybernetics (CIIRC), CTU. My research was awarded with GoodAI grant, where I focused on the Emergence of Novelty in Evolutionary Algorithms. I have also developed completely new supervised technique SPE for sentence embedding in textual adversarial attacks outperforming the state-of-the-art.
Recently, I have joined Alquist AI team to compete in the Alexa Prize Socialbot Grand Challenge.
