Hi, I’m David – co-founder of BottleCap AI, where we make large-language models 100 × faster and cheaper.

During my PhD I co-founded an AI-trading startup with Tomas Mikolov, applying language models to beat the markets, and published work at top conferences. Before that I pushed the boundaries of conversational AI as a finalist in Amazon’s Alexa Prize Grand Challenge.
